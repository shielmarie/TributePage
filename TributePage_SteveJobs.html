TIME LINE OF STEVE JOBS LIFE:
  • 1955: Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was born on February 25, 1955 in San Francisco, California.
  • 1968-1972: He attended Homestead High School.
    • 1972: He attended Reed College.
  • 1972- 1985: Pre-Apple and Apple Time.
  • 1985- 1997: Establishment of NeXT Computer, Pixar, and joining Disney.
  • 1997-2011: Return to Apple
  • 2003: He was diagnosed with cancer.
  • 2011: Jobs died on October 5, 2011 at Palto, California because of complications from a relapse of his previously treated islet-cell neuroendocrine pancreatic cancer, resulting in respiratory arrest.
